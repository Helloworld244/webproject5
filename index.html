Favorite YouTube Channels

A Relaxing Time Just For Men

I love this video because Jenna shows the funny and real thoughts women have when thinking about using hair dye at home instead of going to a salon. Jenna and her partner Julian team up in this video. Jenna starts by trying to dye her eyebrows, sharing her thoughts and making us laugh with her funny comments. Julian helps out and adds to the fun. Their great teamwork and jokes make the video enjoyable and entertaining.

Source: Jenna Marbles

Clueless White Guy Orders in Perfect Chinese, Shocks Patrons and Staff

I love the Xiaomanyc channel because he amazes people with his fluent speaking in different languages and gets great reactions. His videos make you feel connected and inspire you to learn new languages to talk to more people.

Source: Xiaomanyc

How to Open a Can without Can Opener - Zombie Survival Tips #20

Crazy Russian Hacker is hilarious, but he's also serious about his videos. I love his opening catchphrase, 'Safety is number one priority,' which makes his channel memorable and keeps me coming back.
